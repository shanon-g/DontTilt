# Don't Tilt! 

Welcome to the official information page for **Don't Tilt!**, a physical, motion-based balancing game for iOS.

## Privacy Policy

**Shanon Giuly Istanto** built the *Don't Tilt!* app as a Free app. This service is provided at no cost and is intended for use "as is". 

**Data Collection & Usage:**
This app **does not** collect, store, track, or share any personal user data, location data, or device metrics. 

All physical gameplay mechanics and motion tracking (using the device's accelerometer and gyroscope) happen entirely locally on your device in real-time. No motion data or personal information is ever saved or transmitted to external servers.
